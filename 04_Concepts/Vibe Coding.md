# Vibe Coding

## Working definition
Vibe coding is the style of AI-assisted building where intuition, speed, and direct iteration dominate over explicit structure, formal specs, or rigorous validation.

## Core synthesis
The corpus does not treat vibe coding as simply good or bad. It treats it as a phase-appropriate mode with sharp boundaries. For exploration, prototypes, and discovery, vibe coding can unlock speed and energy. But once stakes rise, the lack of explicit constraints becomes expensive. The most recurrent story is not "never vibe code" but "do not mistake vibe coding for production engineering."

That makes vibe coding useful as a contrast concept. It clarifies what mature AI engineering adds: specs, harnesses, evals, repository hygiene, and review discipline. In other words, vibe coding is what the field is trying to grow beyond without losing its creative velocity.

## What this concept is really about
- The tradeoff between velocity and reliability.
- When improvisation is productive versus dangerous.
- Why cheap code generation can hide expensive downstream cleanup.
- The transition from playful making to dependable engineering.

## Recurring patterns in the corpus
1. **Vibe coding is great for search.** It broadens solution exploration.
2. **It fails at durability.** Informal decisions become hard to review and maintain.
3. **Taste becomes central.** Someone still has to recognize quality.
4. **The hangover is real.** Teams eventually pay for weak structure.
5. **The winning move is controlled vibes.** Keep the speed, add scaffolding.

## Important distinctions
### Vibe coding vs vibe engineering
Vibe coding prioritizes rapid generation. Vibe engineering adds system constraints, evaluation, and maintainability.

### Creativity vs slop
The problem is not experimentation. The problem is shipping unexamined output into environments that require trust.

## Design implications
- Use vibe coding deliberately in low-stakes exploration.
- Introduce specs, tests, and review gates before code becomes shared infrastructure.
- Make cleanup and formalization an explicit transition step.
- Teach teams to preserve creative speed without skipping validation.

## Why it matters for the book
This concept gives the book an accessible opening contrast. Readers immediately understand the difference between playful prompting and engineered delegation.
