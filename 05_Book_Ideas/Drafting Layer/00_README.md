# Drafting Layer

This folder adds the next writing layer on top of the existing outline and chapter packets for **From Copilot to Colleague**.

## What is here
- `Quote Bank - Strongest Chapters.md` — source-backed quotes for the strongest technical chapters.
- `Recurring Case Studies.md` — two reusable case studies designed to recur across multiple chapters.
- `Chapter Starters - Chapters 3 4 6.md` — writing-ready chapter starters for the current strongest three chapters.
- `Chapter Starters - Chapters 5 and 9.md` — writing-ready starters for the strongest next-tier infrastructure and organization chapters.
- `Chapter Starter - Chapter 7 Security Identity and High-Stakes Trust.md` — writing-ready starter for the trust/governance chapter.
- `Chapter Starter - Chapter 8 Realtime and Voice.md` — writing-ready starter for the realtime/voice stress-test chapter.

## Editorial choice made here
For this round, the **strongest three chapters** are treated as:
1. Chapter 3 — Harnesses, Specs, and Codebases Agents Can Actually Use
2. Chapter 4 — Evals Are the Control System
3. Chapter 6 — Runtimes, State, and the Human Control Plane

Why these three:
- they form the densest practical core of the book
- they have the clearest source clusters
- they are the most ready to move from synthesis into actual draft prose

Chapter 1 remains strong and important for framing, but it still feels slightly more editorial than these three, which now have a sturdier how-it-works backbone.
