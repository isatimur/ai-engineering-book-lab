## Strongest claims
1. AI-native advantage comes from operating-model redesign, not just seat licenses.
2. The highest leverage organizations broaden who can create while tightening review and governance.
3. Traditional measures of productivity, role boundaries, and compensation are under pressure.
4. Product management and internal platforms become more important as execution cost falls.
5. Team alignment becomes more fragile when individuals direct many agents in private workflows.
6. The org needs shared planning and review spaces, not just better personal agent tools.
