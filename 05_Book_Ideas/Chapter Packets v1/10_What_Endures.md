## Role in the book
Close by separating durable principles from temporary tools. The aim is to leave the reader with an operating model that survives model churn.

## Strongest claims
1. Tool churn is real, but core engineering principles persist.
2. The enduring pattern is constrained delegation backed by context, evals, and human judgment.
3. AI changes interfaces and economics faster than it changes the need for systems thinking.
4. The winning teams will separate stable principles from transient frameworks.
