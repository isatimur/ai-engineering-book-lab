## Overview

Brian Scanlan leads engineering at Intercom, one of the companies most publicly committed to AI-native product transformation. ## Key claims / positions

- Intercom engineering teams achieved approximately 2x throughput increase with AI tooling — but only after deliberate process change, not just tool adoption.
- Tool access alone does not produce the throughput gain; changed workflows, review practices, and task decomposition patterns are what move the needle.
- The productivity gain is not evenly distributed — senior engineers extract more value than junior ones from AI augmentation.
- Measurement is the prerequisite: you cannot manage AI-augmented throughput without baseline metrics.
