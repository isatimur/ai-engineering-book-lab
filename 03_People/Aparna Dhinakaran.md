## Key claims / positions

- Prompt optimization (systematic, eval-driven) outperforms intuition-driven prompt engineering by a measurable margin.
- Self-improving agents require a complete loop: trace collection → failure classification → prompt/data update → re-evaluation.
- Observability is not optional — without traces you cannot close the improvement loop.
- "Break it 'til you make it": deliberate adversarial testing of agents before production is cheaper than reactive fixes after.
